Access information

The internet helps students and teachers provide vast resources of information including textbooks, research papers, articles, and educational videos on virtually any topic. This allows for more research and learning opportunities.

Collaboration and Communication

The internet facilitates communication and collaboration among students, teachers, and researchers. Online platforms and tools make it easier to work together on projects, share resources, and communicating virtually.

Global Connections

The internet allows students to connect with teachers, and experts worldwide. They can join online disciussion, attend webinars, or experience virtual field trips. this enables students to explore various cultures, viewpoints, and way of thinking, making their education more global and diverse.

Online Learning

Online learning is one of the biggest changes that the internet has made. Now students can take courses online and learn at their own pace, no matter where they are. Website like coursera, Khan Academy, and YouTube have videos and lessons that help students understand subjects better. This is really helpful for those who need more time to study or want to learn more about a topic.

Coding Language

coduing lanuage provides children with a systematic approach to tasks and helps them to work through problems in a step by step manner. It is the language that tells your web browser how to structure a web page by defining headers, paragraphs, links, images, and more.
